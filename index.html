Star Zone – Conquer the Galaxy,
Command Your Fleet!

Explore the unknown, build your fleet, and engage in intergalactic battles!

About the Game

  • Step into the boundless universe of Star Zone, a sci-fi strategy game where you take command of a fleet and forge your destiny among the stars!
  • Embark on deep-space missions, construct powerful starships, and battle rival factions in a galaxy teeming with cosmic wonders and hidden dangers.
  • Customize your fleet, harness advanced technology, and shape the future of space exploration. Are you ready to leave your mark on the universe?

Development Challenges:

  • Ensuring balanced gameplay between exploration, combat, and resource management.
  • Creating a vast, immersive galaxy with diverse planets and celestial bodies.
  • Designing compelling narratives that evolve based on player choices.

How to Play

  • Assemble Your Fleet – Choose and upgrade spaceships with specialized abilities.

  • Expand Your Empire – Colonize planets, mine resources, and develop advanced technologies.

  • Engage in Battles – Strategically combat enemies in thrilling real-time space warfare.

  • Forge Alliances – Join forces with other commanders or compete for dominance.

  • Discover the Unknown – Uncover lost civilizations, rare artifacts, and deep-space anomalies.

Game Features

  • Strategic Combat
